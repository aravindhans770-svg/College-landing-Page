What you'll study

Four schools, one studio culture.

Every program is built around a working studio — small cohorts, named instructors, and a portfolio instead of a transcript.

School of Computing

Software engineering, AI systems, and security — taught through shipped products, not slideware.

School of Engineering

Mechanical, electrical, and robotics — with a fabrication floor open to first-years.

School of Design

Product, interaction, and industrial design grounded in research and material craft.

School of Data & Business

Analytics, venture, and operations for people who want to build the company, not just the code.

Research Labs

Twelve active labs spanning robotics, climate systems, and applied machine learning.

Industry Studio

Year-round paid studio partnerships with 60+ companies, embedded directly in coursework.

Why TechNova

A campus built around making, not lecturing.

Lecture halls exist, but the center of gravity is the studio floor — open 24 hours, stocked with the same tools used at the partner companies our graduates join.

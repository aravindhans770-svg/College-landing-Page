How to apply.

We read every application individually — no algorithmic cutoffs on test scores. Here's exactly what we ask for and when.

The process

Four steps, six weeks.

Most applicants hear back within six weeks of submitting a complete file. Here's what happens at each stage.

Start Application
01

Submit your file

Application form, transcripts, and a portfolio or project sample — code, design work, or a build log all count.

02

Studio review

A faculty member from your intended program reviews your portfolio directly — not an admissions-only committee.

03

Conversation

A 20-minute call, in person or remote, about what you want to build and why TechNova fits.

04

Decision

Admit, waitlist, or decline, with specific written feedback either way.
